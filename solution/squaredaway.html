Author’s Notes

The main mechanic of removing letters and re-squaring actually came to me in a dream! Unfortunately, it was a difficult time realizing said dream. I had to find words that fit the categories, and formed the relevant semaphore, and weren't too long (especially hard for the smaller grids), and that had the correct total letters. At least balancing all that taught me an important lesson in not constraining myself too much.
