Solution: Stained Glass
Answer: HARPSICHORDS

Written by quatrevingtneuf

Solvers should start by printing and cutting out the given shape. After making the correct folds and gluing the indicated faces together, solvers should end up with what appears to be a 2x2 square with some extra layers.

By playing around with this shape and considering the flavourtext, we eventually come to the realization that this isn't just any square of paper - it's a flexagon! By folding and unfolding, solvers can arrive at four different pairs of faces.

While flexing through each pair of faces, solvers can compare the shapes on either side of the shape. The polygons only match in a few places on each 4x4 sub-grid, and no matching polygons repeat in any sub-grid. Moreover, there is always a consecutive sequence of matched polygons. With this information, we can start connecting the cells with the matched polygons in order. Each 4x4 sub-grid yields a letter, for a total of 16 letters.

This phrase, PLUCKED KEYBOARDS, clues our final answer, HARPSICHORDS, which may once have been commonly found in buildings with stained glass.
